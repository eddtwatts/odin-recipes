Banana Cake

Home

Description

Our original Banana Cake recipe, made from old banana's.

This is very simple and yummy and we've used it since Phoebe was a baby.

Ingredients

  • 100g self-raising flour
  • 1/2 tsp ground mixed spice
  • 50g butter
  • 75g raisins
  • 2 mendium overripe bananas
  • 50g walnuts (optional)
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180C
  2. Sift flour and spice together
  3. Add butter using rubbing method
  4. Add raisins/walnuts
  5. Mix together wet ingredients
  6. Add wet ingredients
  7. Turn head down to 160C and put in oven
  8. Cook for 45-60 min
